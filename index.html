About Me

Hey! My name is Jeff.

When I was eight years old, my mom decided that I should pick up the violin or piano. I didn't know what either of those words meant, so I went with the one I heard first. Thanks, mom!

Somehow one thing led to another, and I found myself traveling the world performing in places like Costa Rica's National Theater and Taiwan's Presidential Office. Back at home in Los Angeles, I've played in everything from short films and Broadway musicals to string quartets and orchestras, including an associate principal position with the UCLA Symphony.

More recently, I've been on a mission to learn any instrument I can get my hands on (so far: violin, viola, cello, double bass, trombone, and piano) and to team up with couples and planners to provide live performances for life's most important moments (which is probably why you're here!)

When I'm not making music, you'll probably find me attempting the day's NYT Crossword, hosting a game of Blood on the Clocktower, or working my day job as a web designer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Bookings

How is my quote calculated?

My hourly rates are posted above, but your quote may vary from this based on a few factors, primarily the location of your venue and whether you have any custom song requests outside of my repertoire.

To get a personalized quote, please don't hesitate to reach out!

When/how do you collect payment?

Once we've agreed on a quote, I'll send over a contract, at which point a 25% deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remainder of your balance will be due by the day of the performance.

I accept payments through Venmo, Zelle, or cash.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Your 25% deposit will be forfeited in the event of a cancellation.

I am happy to reschedule bookings at no cost, provided the new date and time still works for me.

How far in advance do I need to book?

I will gladly consider all bookings, regardless of how near or far into the future the occasion is! That being said, most bookings I accept tend to be made 2–12 months in advance.

Are you willing to travel?

While most of my bookings are in Southern California, I am open to performing anywhere. An additional fee for gas, airfare, and/or lodging will apply for long-distance bookings.

Is there a minimum/maximum booking length?

I have a minimum booking length of one hour.

While I do not have a strict maximum booking length, for bookings of 2 hours or longer, I reserve the right to take a 5–10 minute break after each hour of playing, during which I'll play canned music through my sound setup.

Music

What's a viola (and why should I care)?

Great question! Imagine if the violin and the cello had a way cooler middle sibling. Due to the viola's unique construction, it resonates differently from other string instruments, resulting in a sound that carries the elegance of a violin and the warmth of a cello, all in one package.

Compared to many other instruments, there aren't a lot of us viola players out there, so booking one is a great way to make your wedding or event stand out! ;)

Can you learn a song for my event?

Yes! If you don't see what you're looking for in my repertoire, I am always happy to learn something new. A small additional fee will apply.

How many songs can I choose?

As many or as few as you'd like! You are welcome to select enough music to fill up the entire length of your booking. But if you'd like some help topping off your setlist, or if you just have a general vibe in mind, I'd be happy to work with you to fill in the gaps based on my recommendations.

Do you take requests from guests?

Yes! However I will only accept requests that I feel confident that I feel I can perform at a sufficiently high level of quality on the spot.

Can you play with a group?

Yes! I can play in a duet, trio, or quartet with other strings and/or a piano by request. Please be advised that this will come at a higher cost, and some songs in my repertoire may not be available.

Can you play a different instrument?

Yes! While I do most of my gigs on viola, I am also comfortable performing on violin and cello.

I am currently piloting a "one-man band" experience involving switching off on different string instruments over the course of one performance. Please reach out if you're interested—as this is a new offering I am happy to provide a discount on your booking!
